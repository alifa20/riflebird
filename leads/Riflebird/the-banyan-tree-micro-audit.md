### Issue 3: Weak Product Page Optimization Killing Click-Through Rates
**Problem:** Poor meta descriptions and thin content on category pages

**Evidence:**

- Homepage meta: Doesn't mention key brands (Ethnicraft, Molmic, Kuatro Living)
- Minimal SEO-optimized content on collection pages (just 2 paragraphs on sofas page)

**Impact:**

**Fix potential:** HIGH - just needs proper copywriting, no technical changes required
