# SEO Micro-Audit: Bathware Direct

**Website:** bathwaredirect.com.au
**Date:** November 2025
**Business Type:** E-commerce (Bathroom & Kitchen Supplies)
**Location:** Australia-wide delivery

---

## Executive Summary

Bathware Direct has experienced a **78% traffic decline** from its peak of ~57,500 monthly visitors in May/June 2024 to just 12,800 today. Despite having decent domain authority (DR 33) and 787 referring domains, the site is losing ground to competitors. Critical on-page SEO issues including missing H1 tags and weak title tags are likely contributing to the decline.

## Critical On-Page Issues Found

### 1. Weak Homepage Title Tag
- **Current:** "Home | Bathware Direct" (23 characters)
- **Problem:** Wastes 80% of available space, no keywords, no value proposition
- **Business Translation:** "Your Google listing says almost nothing about what you sell"
- **Fix:** "Bathroom Vanities, Tapware & Toilets | Free Shipping | Bathware Direct" (~70 chars)

### 2. Missing H1 Tags on Category Pages
- **Issue:** Toilet Suites category page has NO H1 heading tag
- **Impact:** Google doesn't understand the main topic of the page
- **Business Translation:** "Your category pages don't tell Google what products they contain"

### 3. Meta Descriptions Not Rendering
- **Issue:** Meta descriptions not visible in DOM - likely missing or broken
- **Impact:** Google writes your search result descriptions for you (often poorly)
- **Business Translation:** "You're not controlling what customers see in Google results"

### 4. Newsletter Popup Immediately on Load
- **Issue:** Popup appears instantly before users can browse
- **Impact:** Increases bounce rate, hurts user experience signals
- **Business Translation:** "Visitors might leave before seeing your products"
