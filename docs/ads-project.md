PMAX Campaign Manager - Project Overview
What We're Building
A dead-simple Google Ads dashboard that lets non-experts manage Performance Max campaigns without needing to understand all the complexity of Google Ads. Think of it as "Google Ads for humans" - specifically focused only on PMAX campaigns.

The Big Problem We're Solving
Current situation:

Small agencies and business owners struggle with Google Ads - it's overwhelming
The Google Ads interface has hundreds of options, most irrelevant to PMAX
Setting up a PMAX campaign requires understanding conversion tracking, asset groups, audience signals, bid strategies, etc.
People either hire expensive agencies or give up

Our solution:

Strip away 90% of Google Ads complexity
Use an AI chatbot to guide campaign creation (feels like talking to an expert)
Show only the metrics that actually matter
Make it impossible to mess up


Who Is This For?

Small marketing agencies (2-10 people) managing ads for clients but lacking Google Ads experts
Business owners who want to run their own ads but find Google Ads intimidating
Marketing coordinators who got thrown into managing ads without training
Anyone who just wants their PMAX campaigns to work without a PhD in digital marketing

Key Design Principles
1. Conversational Over Forms
Traditional software makes you fill out forms. We let you chat with an AI that asks questions naturally and explains everything.
2. Show Only What Matters
Google Ads has 200+ metrics. We show the 6 that actually matter for PMAX. Everything else is hidden or automated.
3. Explain Like I'm Five
No jargon. Instead of "CPM" we say "cost per 1000 impressions." Tooltips everywhere.
4. Make Fixes Easy
See a problem? Fix it right there with one click. No digging through menus.
5. Color-Coded Everything

Green = good (high ROAS, on budget)
Red = bad (low performance, overspending)
Yellow = warning (declining, needs attention)
